SignalFrame Startup

As technical co-founder and CTO of SignalFrame (founded in 2015, acquired by PwC in 2021), I led a team of 20 engineers in building a streaming temporal graph platform that processed data from ~1 billion IoT devices monthly. Our daily stream consisted of ~100 million vertices with ~1 billion edges. On top of this knowledge graph we implemented advanced graph embeddings for temporal and spatial analytics. Our products focused on behavioral anomaly detection and structural forecasting.
